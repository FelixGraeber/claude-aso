# Health & Fitness ASO Template

## Key Metrics
- Average conversion: 25-35%
- Average rating threshold: 4.4+
- Seasonal peaks: January (resolutions), March-May (summer prep)

## Keyword Themes
- Activity terms: workout, exercise, running, yoga, meditation, sleep
- Goal terms: weight loss, muscle, calories, steps, habits
- Feature terms: tracker, timer, planner, coach, personal trainer
- Integration terms: Apple Health, Google Fit, wearable, watch

## Screenshot Strategy
- Real workout/activity demonstrations
- Progress tracking and goal visualization
- Achievement/streak celebrations
- Watch/wearable companion views
- Before/after or transformation hints

## Monetization-ASO Alignment
- Free trials prominent in metadata
- Seasonal promotions in January (New Year's resolution)
- Emphasize personalization and AI coaching

## Category Competition
High — very seasonal. Time metadata updates to January and spring peaks.
